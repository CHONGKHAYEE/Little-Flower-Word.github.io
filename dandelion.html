Dandelion

Dandelion can be said to be a relatively common flower. Every summer, you can always see dandelion flowers blooming everywhere. Moreover, dandelion is also a kind of Chinese medicinal material. The flower language of dandelion is love that cannot stay.

The flower language of the dandelion:

Unstoppable love, Never-ending love

Many people should have seen dandelion. Dandelion is a kind of vibrant yellow flower, cheerful and charming. Dandelion is always flying freely in the air with good wishes, looking for a new world and a new beginning that belongs to it, just like you have found true love, it is a new starting point and the beginning of a new life for yourself. Dandelion is free again, flying freely with the wind. Therefore, its love is also free. So, what is the flower language of dandelion?

Because dandelions are always floating in the air, only occasionally stay for a while, they just drift away with the wind until they are blown into the soil, grow again, and reproduce. Therefore, dandelion represents love that cannot stay, and it feels bleak. There are also some rare varieties of dandelion, such as red dandelion. Its flower language is the end of the world and will last until death, symbolizing loyalty to love. The dandelion in the memory is quiet, always waiting quietly in the corner of the green flowers, or being blown away by the wind, and then wandering, wandering.

Flower language of different colors of dandelions :

White: Love that never ends

Red: The country thirsting for happiness.
It represents love and unrealistic love, which will last until death.

Purple: Lonely love.
It is said that whoever can find purple dandelion can get perfect love. So what is the flower language of dandelion? It is actually lonely love. However, there is no purple dandelion. It actually refers to thistle. The two are similar. Pay attention to the difference.
