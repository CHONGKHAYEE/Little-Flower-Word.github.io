Daisy Flower

The daisy flower is a small white flower that grows beside the road. The daisy flower is very beautiful.
The heart of the daisy flower is like a small sun, and the petals are pure white. When the daisy flower is in full bloom, it looks like a girl's bright smiling face in the sun.
You will feel better when you see the daisy flower. Daisy flowers can also be said to be healing flowers.

The flower language of the daisy flower:

Happy forever, Do you love me or not, Parting, The forbearing love that cannot be confessed, The love that is deep in my heart

What is the flower language of the daisy flower?
Since ancient times, daisy has been used to divination love affairs. With the petals peeled off one by one, silently say "Love me, don't love me" in my heart until the last petal, which represents the love of the lover. Therefore, its flower language is "innocent." Those who are born under the blessing of this kind of flowers will retain their innocent and innocent heart as a girl throughout their lives.
