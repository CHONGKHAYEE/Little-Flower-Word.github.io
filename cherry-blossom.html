Cherry Blossom

Every spring, many people think of cherry blossoms. April is the season of cherry blossoms. In Japan, there are more than 200 varieties of cherry blossoms from wild to cultivated.

The flower language of the cherry blossom:

Life, Happiness, Never give up for life, Only love you for life, represents Elegant, Simple and Pure love

Many people want to know what is the flower language of cherry blossom. In fact, in many people’s minds, cherry blossom is a symbol of beauty and romance. Therefore, cherry blossom has always been a symbol of love and hope, representing elegance, simplicity and purity of love.

Everyone knows that the cherry blossoms are like ignorant girls, and they open in spring quietly. The white and pink cherry blossoms full of trees are the most beautiful language for telling love to lovers. Someone in my heart, like that lonely cherry blossom rain, slowly disappears into the depths of time, leaving an eternal memory.

Everyone knows that sakura is Japan’s national flower, and March 15 is Japan’s Cherry Blossom Festival. The Japanese believe that life is short, and that life will be as brilliant as cherry blossoms. Even if it is dead, it should be decisively left. When the cherry blossoms fall, they are not polluted and untainted, so they are regarded as a symbol of the Japanese spirit. The Japanese not only enjoy cherry blossoms, they also eat cherry blossoms, and their cherry blossom ice cream is also unique.

There is actually another saying about the symbolism of the flower language of cherry blossoms, which is there is another kind of cherry blossom called Western cherry blossoms. The language of flower is a kind of education. This flower language comes from the education history of the famous American President Washington, because he told his father very honestly: "I cut off the cherry tree." So Western cherry blossoms imply kind education.
