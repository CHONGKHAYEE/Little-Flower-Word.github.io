Roses

Roses have always been a bouquet that symbolizes love.Young couples will choose roses to send each other.The flower language of roses is brave love.

In fact, different rose colors have different flower languages.

Red roses flower language:

Passionate, love you, I love you, passionately, hope to have passionate love with you.

Orange-red roses flower language:

First love mood, friendship, youthful beauty, shyness, a mysterious love for you hope to have passionate love with you.

Blue roses flower language:

Honest, kind, pure and mellow love, miracles and impossible things,
also represent things that can never be obtained, precious and rare love.

Blue Rose Teaser Flower Language:
I have a crush on you, but I can't open my mouth. I think about you every day, do you think of me too. I will let you go one day.
It is also said to be "Melancholy, love in my heart, precious and rare love."

Pink roses flower language:

Touching, declaration of love, first love remembered in my heart, like your bright smile.

White roses flower language:

White roses have always given people the feeling of purity and nobility.
The flower language of white roses: pure, noble, innocent and pure love.

Purple roses flower language:

Purple roses is a variety of roses. The flowers are very small, but the fragrance is very strong.
The flower language of purple roses is only for you in the world.

Black roses flower language:

The feeling that black roses gives us is very noble and mysterious, and black roses is very rare in our lives.
The flower language of black roses is sent to you with tenderness and sincerity.

Green roses flower language:

Maybe many people don’t know much about green roses. Do green roses really exist in the world? The answer is no. Green roses are just a beautiful legend in Israel. Green roses are called flowers that only exist in the sky, but This kind of flower is endowed with many beautiful meanings.
The flower language of green rose is pure and simple, youthful, and never-old love
