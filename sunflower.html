Sun Flower

Sunflower is a kind of flower that represents sunshine and vitality in many people's hearts.

The flower language of the sun flower:

Silent love, Unspoken love, Admiration, Brilliance, and Loyalty

Sunflower's name because the inflorescence rotation with the sun named. Because the sunflower has phototropism, people call it a sunflower, a flower that revolves with the sun. Moreover, in the ancient Inca Empire, the sunflower was a symbol of the sun god. So what is the flower language of sunflower?

In fact, the flower language of sunflower is faith, brilliance, arrogance, loyalty, and admiration. The meaning of sunflower is silent love, which represents the brave pursuit of the happiness you want. The sunflowers that follow the sun are very popular. Those born under the blessing of sunflowers have a bright and happy heart like the sun and are the best life-long companions.

Although the flower position of a sunflower is not as romantic as a rose or as pure as a lily, it is sunny, bright, and magnanimous in love. It has its own unique charm. Moreover, it blooms not only love, but also love. There is a love for dreams and life.

Flower language of different numbers of sunflowers :

5 sunflowers: I have never regretted loving you, even if I never say it, but my love is never less than anyone else.

6 sunflowers: write happiness in a lifetime, savor the sunshine of a lifetime.

9 sunflowers: The mood should be like midsummer, so brilliant, so full, no one can stop.

10 Sunflowers: Open every day, I hope there is a ray of sunshine to make you happy.

30 sunflowers: I hope you can accept my love, I don't want to be silent anymore.
